A B O U T

EYESHADOW 2600 FM started in 2017 out of Harrisburg, Pennsylvania as a single-artist act and a very experimental learning experience that eventually morphed into the darksynth monster you can hear today.

ES2600FM is simultaneously inspired by an eighties and horror aesthetic, the sounds of a decade of a bygone era, and a sprawling headcanon of a furious bloodsucking vampire trapped an isolated bubble of time and space, her life repeating over and over again in cycles that evolve with each passing year--A long-running piece of fiction meant for paperback, twisted into a visage represented through grinding synths and sparkling tones. A push-and-pull dynamic that constantly shifts between an apocalyptic wasteland, a cyperpunk future cityscape and the choking vacuum of intergalactic deep space.

This is Eyeshadow 2600 FM, a melting pot of ideas, shaped into one ever-expanding set piece that never dies.
